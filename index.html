National Health Policy :

  1. Diseases are divided in 3 groups
    1. Diseases which have one time treatment
    2. Diseases which can be treated by proper food diet or regular medications
    3. Disease which require lifelong treatment. As this is a special health care domain its complete authority will be towards the central government to maintain a centralised system and to avoid any kind of discrepancies.
  1. The economic help for the treatment and the medications for the diseases in the first group will be provided by the central government and the economic help will be upto Rs.10 crores(The help can be extended upto Rs.15 crores for exceptional cases).
  1. For group 2 diseases, the central government will appoint the specialised doctors for proper food diet and regular medications for a group of people suffering from the same disease and will assist the 75% of the total cost of the treatment.
  1. For group 3 diseases, the government will transfer a reasonable amount in the bank accounts of the respective person or the relatives of the person and will provide medicines at very low cost at the government centres.
  1. The government will also set up at least one research and development lab in each state to make it possible to develop medicines which are currently required to be imported from the other countries.
  1. As the patient fills the application form for the economic and medical assistance from the government, a medical representative will visit the patient within 48 hours to check the reality and understand the case and will submit the report to the higher authority for the approval within 48 hours and will get response from the higher authority within 72 hours of submission of the report. If not received any of the above mentioned follow up from the government centre then the patient may call the grievance centre and the issue will be solved within 24 hours.
  1. The Government will select the manufacturing companies for manufacturing the medicines and will also assist the manufacturers by withdrawing the import taxes on the import of the raw material.
